# Research Methodology

## Topic
AI Powered SEO Content Production

## Selection Criteria

Experts were chosen on the basis of:

- Active Publishing in SEO & AI Workflows
- Showcased real rorld SEO rxperience
- Consistent and educational content on LinkedIn, Youtube, podcasts, or blogs
- Have indsutry recognitiona and influence

## Research Process

- Identify leading SEO professionals talking about AI content production.
- Collect recent LinkedIn posts and YouTube content.
- Organize content by source and author.
- Analyze recurring themes, workflows, and best practices.
- Prepare material and structure findings in a way for future playbook (step-by-step guide) development.
